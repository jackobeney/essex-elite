About Us

Essex Elite football coaching was established in 2012 by co-founders Billy and Joe. Between them they have almost 15 years of coaching experience, which includes coaching Premier League and League Two Football Clubs. They have both played football to a semi professional standard for local teams and have a good knowledge of the local youth football set up.
